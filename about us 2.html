About Us

We bake cakes and cookies in different flavours to relish the finest taste of your choice. We follow traditional and new recipes to enrich the taste of all ages. All organic ingradients are used to not only enhance the taste but to provide healthy food.

Ras malai dessert consists of flattened balls of chhena soaked in malai (clotted cream) flavoured with cardamom. It is enriched with saffron colour and flavour. Dry fruits like pistachios and almonds add an additional taste.
